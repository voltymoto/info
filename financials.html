Investment Highlights

Market Opportunity

  • HCMC gasoline ban on delivery & ride-hailing vehicles creates urgent transition timeline
  • 400K+ unit TAM across delivery fleets and ride-hailing through 2029
  • BaaS infrastructure expanding; proven customer demand in pilot discussions
  • First-mover advantage as cross-compatible platform (competitors locked into single ecosystems)

Business Model Strength

  • Capital-light: Partners fund infrastructure; NUEN captures vehicle + subscription margin
  • Multi-partner hedge: No single-customer concentration risk
  • Recurring revenue: Service subscriptions + accessories provide margin stability
  • Proven partnerships: Signed MOUs with Selex, Power-GOGO, Zeen; Be Group advanced talks

Financial Trajectory

  • Cash-positive Month 12 with base case $4.4M cumulative 24-month flow
  • Gross margin expansion: 2.4% (pilot) → 6.7% (scale) → 15.4% (optimistic)
  • EBITDA reaches 18%+ at full production scale (5K+ annual units)
  • Self-funding post-Month 12 via 50% customer deposit model + positive operational cash

Execution De-Risk

  • Phased tooling: $20K soft tooling validates design before $400K scale commitment
  • Milestone gates: Mid-scale tooling funding released only post-pilot validation
  • Customer deposit model: 50% upfront payment minimizes working capital gap vs. OEM suppliers
  • Clear homologation path: Vietnam Register type approval in progress (M1–3)
